<section class="home-section" id="about">

<p>Hi, I'm <strong>Pratik</strong> — a Research Engineer at <a href="https://ai.meta.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Meta</strong></a> in New York, working on foundation models for Instagram's recommendation systems. My work spans large-scale sequential modeling, multimodal architectures, and real-time ranking systems serving billions of users.</p>

<p>Before Meta, I completed my Master's at <strong>UC Berkeley</strong> (MIMS, ML focus), interned at <strong>Amazon Science</strong> on graph-based recommendation systems, and spent three years as an ML/Software Engineer at <strong>JPMorgan Chase</strong> and <strong>Tingtun AS</strong> (Norway).</p>

<p>I'm interested in building intelligent systems that operate at scale — where model quality, efficiency, and real-world impact are all first-class concerns.</p>
